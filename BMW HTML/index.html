The Legend Reborn

The BMW M5 CS is a limited edition high-performance sedan that defines the pinnacle of the M division. It is the most powerful M5 of all time, loaded with special M performance parts. Available for select markets including North America, South Africa, and Germany, this is a collector's item in the making.

Critics often say modern cars lack soul, but the CS badge changes everything. BMW M has knocked it out of the park, creating a sedan that feels like a pure sports car.

Driving Experience

The BMW M5 CS delivers shattering horsepower. With retuned springs and Dynamic Damper Control, it offers a connection to the road that the standard Competition model hints at but never fully realizes. The four-seat configuration ensures passengers are held tight while you attack the corners.
